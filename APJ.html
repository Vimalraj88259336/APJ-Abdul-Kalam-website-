Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian scientist and politician who served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007.

He was a renowned aerospace engineer and played a key role in India's civilian space program and military missile development. Dr. Kalam was also known as the "Missile Man of India."

Birth Date: October 15, 1931
Death Date: July 27, 2015
